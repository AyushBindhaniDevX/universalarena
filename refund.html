Refund Policy

This Refund Policy applies to the Universal Arena 2024 website and outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds will be processed.

1. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds will be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Product or service is defective
  • Product or service does not meet customer expectations

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, customers must contact our customer support within 30 days of the purchase date. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days and credited to the customer's bank account.

For any questions regarding the Refund Policy, please contact us.
